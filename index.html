About Vultr

Vultr, founded in 2014, is on a mission to empower developers and businesses by simplifying the deployment of infrastructure via its advanced cloud platform. Vultr is strategically located in 20 datacenters around the globe and provides frictionless provisioning of public cloud, storage and single-tenant bare metal.

Vultr has made it our priority to offer a standardized highly reliable high performance cloud compute environment in all of the cities we serve. Launching a cloud server environment in 20 cities around the globe has never been easier!
